My Darling from Afar

By Pauleen Gella B. Torralba

This time of day brings me great dismay

Unexpectedly, a gust of wind hits me on my face

Making my tired eyes suddenly jolt awake

And from a certain distance, there I see his lovely face

It makes me think back to all those times

Where I admired him from close behind

I painted all those memories in the depths of my mind, not wanting a glimpse of it left behind

I want him to help me piece together the puzzle of my fate, trusting his hands to guide the way

For all that I am missing right now is the final piece, to complete the future in store for me

Darling, can't you see that you're the last piece I need?

I hope he can say the same to me

For time is golden, and so is he
